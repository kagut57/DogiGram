Why DogiGram

Everything you love about Telegram

Built on the latest official Telegram for Android source, with its own identity on top.

💬

Full messaging

Private chats, groups, channels, media, files, voice & video calls — the complete Telegram experience.

🔒

Secure by design

Backed by Telegram's MTProto protocol with optional end-to-end encrypted secret chats.

⚡

Fast & lightweight

The same speed you expect, with a snappy, distraction-free interface.

🎨

Dark + violet look

Dark-by-default theme with a signature violet accent and a friendly Shiba mascot.

🐶

Its own identity

Installs side by side with official Telegram, so you can keep both if you want.

🆓

Open source

100% free and open under the GPL — read every line, build it yourself, or contribute.
